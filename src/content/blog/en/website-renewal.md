We are pleased to announce the complete renewal of Acecore's official website.

## Background

Since its founding, Acecore has provided a wide range of services including system development, website production, and server operations. As the business has grown, we felt the need for a website that more clearly communicates our service offerings, leading to this renewal.

The previous site presented content in a feed format, but the new site features dedicated pages for each service and a new blog section, making it easier for clients to quickly find the information they need.

## Features of the New Site

![The new site is built with a modern tech stack](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1467232004584-a241de8bcf5d?w=800&h=400&fit=crop&q=80)

### Fast Loading Speed

The new site uses the **Astro** framework with static site generation (SSG) for fast loading. With global delivery via **Cloudflare Pages**, the site loads smoothly no matter where you access it from.

### Modern Design

We focused on a simple, easy-to-read design. Card-style layouts make each service overview immediately clear, and intuitive navigation keeps things user-friendly. The responsive design ensures comfortable viewing on mobile devices as well.

### Blog

We will publish technical information, announcements, and activity reports in blog format. Stay tuned for insights on Acecore's initiatives and technology trends — be sure to check back regularly.

### SEO and Accessibility

The site is designed with search engine optimization (SEO) and web accessibility in mind. Structured data and semantic HTML improve discoverability through search engines.

## Looking Ahead

With this renewal, we will strengthen the following initiatives:

- **Regular blog updates**: Publishing technical articles, activity reports, and industry trends on a regular basis
- **Case study expansion**: Gradually sharing past project case studies
- **Community information**: Announcing study groups and event information
- **Service page enhancement**: Adding more detailed information for each service

We will continue to enrich our content and share valuable information for our clients and community. Feel free to reach out with service inquiries or partnership discussions.

We look forward to your visit.
