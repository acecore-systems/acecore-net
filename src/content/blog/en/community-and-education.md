At Acecore, we are committed to advancing the tech community and contributing to education under the theme of "connection, discovery, and co-creation."

## Aceserver: A Community Born from Gaming

**Aceserver**, operated by Acecore, is a public Minecraft server used by many players. It functions not only as a place for interaction through gaming, but also as a testing ground for server operations and a space for practicing community management.

The Aceserver [Discord community](https://discord.gg/acsv) hosts active exchanges on topics beyond gaming, including technical discussions and everyday interactions. It features an open atmosphere where anyone can participate regardless of age or experience, from beginners to veterans.

![The Aceserver community fosters interaction regardless of age or experience](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1542751371-adc38448a05e?w=800&h=400&fit=crop&q=80)

The insights gained from server operations are fed back into our enterprise infrastructure services. This has led to the accumulation of practical know-how in performance tuning under high-load environments, DDoS protection, and secure user data management.

## Study Groups and Events

We regularly host technical study groups and workshops to support knowledge sharing and skill development.

- **Programming Hands-on Sessions**: Web app development experiences for beginners. From HTML basics to building a working app in a single day
- **Server Management Introduction**: Linux fundamentals and cloud server setup. Practical content using real environments like AWS and Cloudflare
- **Design Workshops**: Logo creation and UI design basics. From learning design tools to mastering effective visual communication

Participation is free or low-cost and open to everyone. Event information is shared on our blog and social media.

## Acecore Schools Educational Activities

At our IT school "Acecore Schools," active engineers provide one-on-one instruction. The curriculum centers on exercises using real projects, developing practical skills so that "what you learn can be used immediately in the field."

### Curriculum Highlights

1. **Practice-based learning**: Focused on skills demanded in actual development workplaces, not just textbook knowledge
2. **Individually customized**: Curricula flexibly adjusted to match each student's goals and level
3. **Ongoing support**: Technical consultations available not only during the course but also after completion

Students have shared feedback such as "It became clear what I need to do in practice" and "I instantly understood things I couldn't grasp on my own."

![Students learning through a practice-based curriculum](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1509062522246-3755977927d7?w=800&h=400&fit=crop&q=80)

## Contributing to Open Source

At Acecore, we value not only benefiting from open source software but also actively contributing back. We publish some of our internally developed tools and libraries on [GitHub](https://github.com/acecore-systems), giving back to the tech community.

Participating in open source also leads to improved technical skills. Feedback from developers worldwide helps us raise code quality and contributes to the team's skill development.

## Local Community Initiatives

We collaborate with local businesses and educational institutions to promote IT adoption and develop digital talent. By supporting IT adoption in rural areas, we aim to contribute to regional economic development.

Specifically, we conduct IT courses at local schools and vocational colleges, provide DX support for local businesses, and participate in municipal digitalization projects.

For inquiries about community participation or partnership discussions, please [contact us](/contact/). We're always looking forward to welcoming new partners to build something together.
